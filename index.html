Registration Guidelines

  • There is no fee to participate
  • Each public school district may submit up to 5 pieces
  • Each private school may submit up to 1 piece
  • Each after-school program or private studio may submit up to 1 piece
  • Registration must be completed by March 14, 2015 by midnight

Size Limitations

  • No larger than 18" x 18" x 18"
  • Preferably free standing
  • For wall-hanging or free-hanging pieces please email Tricia Barry by March 1st, 2015

Drop-off Information

Teachers must unpack own pieces and place into designated area at exhibit in order to ensure safe handling of pieces.

Packing Information

Each piece must have a discrete label affixed somewhere on vessel including student name and school.

Packaging and boxes will not be saved or stored and will not be provided at pick-up.
